Procedure and Payment

This study consists of {{C.TASKS_PART1_DIRECT}} blocks. In each block, you can earn a bonus payment. At the end of the study, one block will be randomly selected to determine your bonus payment. This is referred to as the "block-that-counts". Your payoff in this block-that-counts will be added to your participation fee and paid out within one week.
Details about the potential bonus payment and instructions will be provided before each block.
