Team Competitions and Bonus

You complete each task individually and receive a score. In each task, team competitions will be held between {{participant.group}}s and {{participant.outgroup}}s. Teams of {{C.TEAM_MEMBERS}} participants who identify as {{participant.group}}s and {{C.TEAM_MEMBERS}} participants who identify as {{participant.outgroup}}s will each compete against each other. The team with the higher score in the task, which is the sum of the individual points, wins. In case of a tie, the winner is determined by a coin flip. Team assignments are made at the end of the study.

Your own performance will also be relevant for the team competitions, as you may be a member of one or more teams.

You can earn a bonus payment. At the end of the study, one of the teams you were a member of will be randomly selected. You will receive a bonus of ${{C.BONUS_WIN}} if this team wins its task competition.
